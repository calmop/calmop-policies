Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This policy explains when refunds are issued and how cancellations are handled for our ecommerce operations services.

By engaging our services, you agree to this policy.

1. No Free Trials

We do not offer free trials.

All services are paid services and begin only after payment and onboarding requirements are completed.

2. Onboarding Fee

A one-time onboarding fee is charged at the start of the engagement.

This fee covers initial setup work, including:

  • Access configuration
  • Workflow setup
  • Policy alignment
  • Ownership handoff

The onboarding fee is non-refundable.

3. Monthly Subscription Fees

All services are billed monthly in advance.

Once a billing cycle has started:

  • Monthly fees are non-refundable
  • No partial refunds are offered for unused time

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are issued only in limited and specific circumstances.

A refund may be issued only if services have not commenced due to a failure on our side after payment and onboarding.

Once operational work has started, subscription fees are non-refundable.

5. 21-Day Ops Fit Guarantee

We offer a 21-Day Ops Fit Guarantee to help determine whether our services are the right fit.

If, within the first 21 days, daily ecommerce operations do not feel clearly off your plate, you may choose not to continue the engagement.

This guarantee:

  • Is not a free trial
  • Does not provide automatic refunds
  • Exists solely to allow you to decide whether to continue

Payments already made remain non-refundable unless covered under Section 4 of this policy.

6. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time by providing notice before your next billing date.

Upon cancellation:

  • Services will continue until the end of the current billing period
  • No further charges will be applied after the current billing cycle

7. Client-Related Delays & Ineligibility for Refunds

Refunds will not be issued for dissatisfaction or delays caused by:

  • Late or incomplete access to required systems
  • Missing, unclear, or changing policies
  • Limited communication or cooperation from the client
  • Operational volume or complexity beyond initial expectations

8. Chargebacks & Payment Disputes

Initiating chargebacks or payment disputes without first contacting us may result in immediate service termination.

Refund eligibility does not extend to chargebacks initiated without prior communication.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Refund & Cancellation Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
