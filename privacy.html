Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Calmop (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides ecommerce operations support services to Shopify-based beauty, skincare, and wellness brands. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our website or engage our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal and business information including:

  • Name, email address, and business details submitted via contact forms
  • Client-provided store access data (Shopify, support inboxes, subscription tools)
  • Operational data required to perform ecommerce support services

2. How We Use Information

Information is used strictly to:

  • Provide agreed ecommerce operations services
  • Communicate with clients
  • Maintain service quality and internal reporting

3. Data Processing Role

Calmop acts as a data processor on behalf of its clients. We do not own, sell, or independently use customer data accessed through client systems.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be accessed only by authorized team members and trusted tools required for service delivery.

5. Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. International Operations

Calmop operates from India and serves clients primarily in the United States and Canada. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of information across borders.
