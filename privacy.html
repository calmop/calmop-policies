Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our website or engage our ecommerce operations services.

We respect your privacy and handle all information with care, discretion, and confidentiality.

a) Information you provide directly

When you become a client, you may grant us access to certain business systems, which may include:

  • Shopify store data

We may use trusted third-party tools to operate our services, including:

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of platforms you authorize us to access.

6. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to:

  • Understand site usage

These tools do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

  • Deliver services

Upon termination of services, access to client systems is removed within a reasonable time.

8. Your Rights

You may request:

Requests can be made by contacting us directly.
